    Bio

    I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate working on AI-augmented engineering design, with interests in generative design, multidiscilinary design optimization (MDO), and scientific machine learning. I am supervised by Prof. Rhea Liem, and I work closely with Prof. Jichao Li and Zhen Wei. I spent a year as a visiting researcher at EPFL’s Computer Vision Lab under Prof. Pascal Fua. Previously, I earned my M.S. from UC San Diego (LSDO Lab, supervised by Prof. John Hwang) and my B.Eng. from Beihang University.

    My goal is to accelerate the design pipelines with a scalable and reliable manner that couple high-fidelity physics to support real-world engineering decisions.
